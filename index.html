Content Management System(CMS) with full version control

A Content Management System allows you to manage your website using a web browser from anywhere with internet connectivity. The content and your website layout templates are stored in a secure online repository(a collection of folders) with a version control system. The CMS connects securely to the repository to allow you to make changes to your website source files. When you save your changes your website is built from the source files and published live to the cloud hosting. The built in version control allows you to easily revert back to a previous version of your website if you inadvertantly publish some incorrect content. Unlike the more commonly used Dynamic CMS systems like wordPress, Joomla! and Drupal your CMS is not hosted with your website nor does it require the use of a complex database management system.

Web Hosting - Scalable and Distributed

Your website will be hosted on a Content Delivery Network(CDN) with Multiple Data Centres around the globe (nodes). Visitors to your website will be served from the node closest to their location resulting in faster page loads. If one node is unable to serve requests, traffic is re-routed to an alternative node. By contrast traditional web hosting serves all traffic from one point which, when traffic spikes, can become unresponsive until the back-log of requests is served or dropped.

In brief your site hosted on a CDN requires almost zero maintenace, is secure, scales well and is always available. A traditional dynamic CMS system hosted on a traditional server is less able to deal with heavy load, is slower to serve pages(it needs to build them first) and requires regular updates and security patches costing the owners time or money.

Getting your website looking right is (not) the most important thing

How do you make the right choices when it comes to launching a new website?

Over more than 20 years, we have seen all the mistakes. People launch a website that talks only about themselves. Others launch a brochure website that never changes and does not help the business marketing efforts. People fall out with their "web guy" and lose control of their domain. To sort out the problem, they get involved with a new "web guy", only to have similar problems with him in the future. We see the brave folk who go it alone with WordPress but, later, regret their choice when they spend far too much time on maintenance. Or others who go with the online drag and drop site builder systems. They can quickly build a site but then, as requirements change, they find that these "one size fits all" packages are not flexible enough. The choice then is to do nothing, pay more to add a feature or manage the hassle of moving their website to another platform.

Unsurprisingly, we have a view on all this.

Most folks start from the wrong place. They obsess about how the website is going to look. Most offerings out there know this and make it ‘2 click’ simple for new customers to get going with a sexy looking template. All template demos have wonderful images and typefaces that make them look extremely appealing. The first thing you need to do with a template is add your own images and your own copy and then look at the template with fresh eyes. Does it look so eye poppingly beautiful with your logo and photos? Does it need to look like the template demo?

Don’t get bogged down with pixel perfect design. A perfect design on your desktop is going to look vastly different on a much smaller smart-phone screen. Not all visitors arrive to your website via the home page. Does it really need to look like a triumphal arch?

Visitors to your website do not have a preconceived idea about what they are going to find. So if your logo is red or blue probably hasn’t crossed their mind. Most visitors don’t care so much about you and your image. Visitors are focused on their own issues and getting a solution.

Your website needs to reflect this. Make information easy to find. Don’t hide your important content in an ocean of clever dancing widgets. Help visitors find what they want quickly and easily and they will repay you with their attention and loyalty.
